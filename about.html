The founder's note

From Vrindavan, for Vrindavan.

Mayra Homes was founded by Atul Pathak with a simple belief — that a home is more than four walls and a deed. It's a relationship between land, family and future. That's why our tagline is "Ghar se badhkar ek rishta".

As an authorized channel partner, Mayra Homes specializes exclusively in premium and affordable real estate projects across the Delhi NCR region and the spiritual hub of Vrindavan. We represent only a small, carefully chosen set of projects — so every client gets personal attention, clean legal clarity, and honest guidance from booking to possession.

Whether you're buying your first plot, planning retirement near the temples of Braj, or diversifying your portfolio with RERA‑compliant land, we'd love to walk you through the options.
