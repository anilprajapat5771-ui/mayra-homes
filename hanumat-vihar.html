The Project

A township built for balanced living.

Hanumat Vihar is conceived as Mathura‑Vrindavan's premier planned township. Developed by Basera Group and approved by the Mathura Vrindavan Development Authority (MVDA), it brings together gated security, wide internal roads, large green parks and educational infrastructure right inside the community.

Plot sizes range from 100 to 360 sq. yds — making it equally suitable for end‑users planning their dream home and investors seeking long‑term appreciation in Vrindavan's fast‑growing real estate corridor.
