The Project

The hi‑tech city Vrindavan deserves.

Suncity Anantam is one of the largest and most ambitious planned townships in the Vrindavan region — spread across three sectors with multiple residential pockets, commercial zones, industrial‑use land, recreational spaces and dedicated facility plots.

Positioned directly on the Delhi‑Agra National Highway (NH‑2) and connected via a 45 m wide approach road, the township offers six plot categories ranging from 117 to 520 sq. yds. Each category is designed for a different end‑user — from first‑time homebuyers to investors seeking premium land parcels.
